Setup in 4 Steps

1

Download & Install

Run SuperChatSetup.exe and follow the installer. No extra software needed.

2

Create Your Account

Open SuperChat from your desktop. Sign up with your streamer username and a password.

3

Minimize to Tray

Click the minimize button — the app hides to your system tray and keeps running silently.

4

Share Your Link

Tell viewers to go to superchat.app/send?to=YourName to send you superchats. Pop-ups appear automatically, even over games.
